Our Services

New, custom & restored vintage bicycles


SoS Bike supplies bicycles with an environmental sustainability vision: reduce bicycle waste.

The team sources old bicycles and parts from the public and business partners and is planning to collaborate with Aarhus municipality.


Get in touch to:

  • buy a new or restored vintage bicycle
  • customize a bicycle
  • donate or trade an old bicycle or parts
  • book a repair or maintenance service

    • We can collect donated items from you.
    You can also trade an old bicyle or parts for a discount on your purchase.
