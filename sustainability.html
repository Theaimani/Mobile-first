Sustainability

Better quality, price & better for the environment

The SoS Bike vision is to reduce bicycle waste.

We source old bicycles and parts, restore them and re-introduce them to the market.

Better for the environment

You get to buy a bicycle that's built of re-used materials. This means less pressure on limited resources and less waste.

Better quality and price

Quality vintage bicycles are not only cheaper but generally have better value and last longer than their new peers.
New bikes are often built with components of inferior quality, leading to rapid deterioration and rust that becomes a problem a few months down the line.
Since this takes time to clean and restore, most bike shops simply replace these components with new parts of a similar quality, and the cycle starts again.

Why do that instead of restoring vintage bikes?
